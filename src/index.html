About Us

Open Patterson is a Code For America Brigade. We are volunteers interested in making Patterson's local government more equitable, transparent and accessible to all peoples using technology and centering people. We are interested in the application of technology and data to serve our communities. We encourage the involvement of collaboration between our local government and all residents. We are students, engineers, advocates, designers, and engaged residents.

Mission Statement

We partner with our local government and local community organizations to build and improve on local services, use data to inform residents, and work towards improving local civic engagement.
